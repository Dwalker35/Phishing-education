This is a friendly reminder that online security is crucial. The following tips can help you avoid falling victim to phishing attacks and other online threats:

Be Aware of Phishing Scams

  • Verify Links: Before clicking on any link in an email or message, hover over it to check if the URL looks legitimate.
  • Check Sender's Email: Ensure that the sender's email address is correct and matches the organization it claims to represent.
  • Be Cautious with Attachments: Do not open attachments from unknown or unexpected sources. They might contain malware.
  • Look for Red Flags: Watch out for urgent or threatening language, spelling errors, or requests for personal information.
  • Use Multi-Factor Authentication: Enable multi-factor authentication on your accounts for added security.
  • Keep Software Updated: Regularly update your operating system, browsers, and security software to protect against vulnerabilities.
  • Educate Yourself: Stay informed about the latest phishing tactics and best practices for online security.
